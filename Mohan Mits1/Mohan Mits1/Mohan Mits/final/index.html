our facilities in MITS

world class library

The library at Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) is a comprehensive resource center that caters to the academic and research needs of students and faculty. It boasts a vast collection of books, journals, periodicals, and magazines across various disciplines, including engineering, technology, management, and science. The library also provides access to numerous digital resources such as e-books, e-journals, and databases like IEEE Xplore, ScienceDirect, and J-Gate, which facilitate advanced research. Well-furnished reading rooms and study areas offer a conducive environment for individual and group study. Additional services include book lending, reference assistance, and interlibrary loans. The library is equipped with computers, internet access, and facilities for photocopying, scanning, and printing. Orientation programs and information literacy sessions are conducted to help users effectively navigate the library's resources. With extended operating hours, the MITS library ensures that students and faculty have ample access to its resources, supporting the institution's mission of providing quality education and fostering research.
